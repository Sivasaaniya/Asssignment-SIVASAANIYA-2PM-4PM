Chennai news

Chennai formerly known as Madras, is the capital of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. Located on the Coromandel Coast off the Bay of Bengal, it is one of the biggest cultural, economic and educational centres in South India. According to the 2011 Indian census, it is the sixth-largest city and fourth-most populous urban agglomeration in India. The city together with the adjoining regions constitute the Chennai Metropolitan Area, which is the 36th-largest urban area by population in the world.

Coimbatore

Coimbatore, the “Manchester of South India,” is a dynamic city blending industrial prowess, cultural heritage, and natural beauty.

Tirupur

Tirupur, Tamil Nadu’s textile titan, is a city where industry meets heritage and nature. It’s globally renowned for its knitwear exports and also offers rich cultural and ecological experiences.

Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of mainland India, is a captivating blend of geography, spirituality, and history. It’s where three seas meet and cultures converge, making it one of Tamil Nadu’s most iconic destinations.
